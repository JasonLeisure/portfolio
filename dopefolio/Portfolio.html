Project 3

Animation that features a captivating visual display where a colorful gradient background shifts through the colors of the rainbow. On top of this backdrop, a canvas element hosts a group of vibrant orbs that move unpredictably across the canvas. These orbs respond to mouse interactions, following the cursor and leaving colorful trails behind them. As they move, the orbs gradually increase in brightness, adding a dynamic and interactive element to the animation, which combines the beauty of the rainbow gradient with the playful behavior of the orbs on the canvas. That motion also randomly paints the orbs.
